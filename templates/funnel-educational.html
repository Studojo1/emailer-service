{{define "content"}} Playbook

The outreach playbook that actually gets replies.

5 things that make the difference between a reply and a ghost:

  • 1
    Be specific. Reference something real about their company or role. Generic messages get ignored.
  • 2
    Keep it short. 3 to 5 sentences max. Respect their time.
  • 3
    Lead with value. What can you bring? Lead with that, not your credentials.
  • 4
    One clear ask. A 15-minute call, a reply, a question. Not a job offer.
  • 5
    Follow up once. 5 to 7 days later. One follow-up doubles your reply rate.

Outreach Dojo applies all five automatically, for every person you target.
