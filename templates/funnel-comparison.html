Your CV joins 200–400 others. An ATS filters 70% before a human sees it. Of the ones that get through, a recruiter spends 6 seconds on each. Most never get a reply.

Here's what happens when you reach out directly:

Your message lands in someone's inbox with their name on it. They read it. If the timing's right, they reply. If it's not, a follow-up in 5 days doubles your chances.
